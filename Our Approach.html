How we invest

  • Atomico is built on the conviction that entrepreneurs are the ultimate gamechangers for positive transformation across our society and economy. Our mission is to support their global progress.

    As such, we are a generalist technology investor, partnering across verticals where we see the most ambitious founders building for positive outcomes.

    For us, mission alignment is the most important thing. We are not here to make incremental progress. We want to accelerate those who move the world forward. No vision is too ambitious - just take a look at our portfolio.

    It’s never too early to talk to us. We partner with companies from the earliest stages to pre-IPO across two funds: Venture and Growth.

    Our dedicated Venture team invests at Series A, alongside occasional Seed opportunities. To meet our Venture investors, click here.

    Our specialised Growth team invests from Series B to pre-IPO. We have a dedicated Growth team to help you scale into global category leaders. To meet our Growth investors, click here.

Commitments We’re Making To Build a Better Ecosystem in Europe

Our mission at Atomico is to develop the world’s most prosperous, sustainable, inclusive and mission-driven technology ecosystem right here in Europe.

Since 2006 we have been building a different kind of venture capital firm. At Atomico, we seek to partner with the world’s most ambitious and mission driven founders: those using technology to rewire the world in favour of something better. When we enter into those partnerships we work together to help them build enduring, category defining businesses that are also conscious about their impact. We recognise, given the wide-reaching impact that some of our portfolio companies can have, the privileged hand we have as a VC in helping build a sustainable future, and the responsibility we carry of ensuring the companies we partner with commit to that.
